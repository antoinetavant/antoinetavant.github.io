Positivity in France

2021-04-08

The Covid data Comes from data.gouv.fr There is a 3 ou 4 days delays, as the data is processed and checkef before beeing updated

The figure is updated every 12h from my Pi4.

The bottom figure allow to compare the evolution from one week to another.

The data shown here is the raw positivty :

$$ P = \frac{N_{\rm positive test} }{ N_{\rm tests}} $$

so unfortunately, the duplicate tests (ie : someone taking 2 tests with a short delay ) are included in the positivty value.
